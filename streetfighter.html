Ducati V4s Streetfighter

Ducati V4s Streetfighter

Ducati Streetfighter V4 is a bike that pushes the concepts of the "Fight Formula" even further. The evolution follows that of the Panigale V4 family: no fairings, high and wide handlebars, 178 kg weight, 208 hp Desmosedici Stradale engine, biplane wings and latest generation electronic package

  • DISPLACEMENT: 1,103 cc
  • POWER: 153 kW (208 hp) @ 13,000 rpm
  • TORQUE: 123 Nm (90.4 lb-ft) @ 9,500 rpm
  • SEAT HEIGHT: 845 mm (33.3 in)
  • WET WEIGHT NO FUEL: 195 kg (430 lb)
  • MAINTENANCE SERVICE INTERVALS: 12,000 km (7,500 mi) / 12 months
  • PRICE: $75,900
